About the Chapter

Rooted in the Four Cardinal Principles

Achievement, Leadership, Scholarship, and Citizenship — the standard Kappa Alpha Psi has carried since 1911, lived out locally by the Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter.

Our Chapter

Alumni brotherhood, active service.

The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter is made up of initiated members of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. living and working across Denton and Lewisville, Texas. We govern our chapter through elected officers, including the Polemarch and the Guide Right Director, and we hold ourselves to the fraternity's national bylaws and standards of conduct alongside our own chapter governance.

  • Chapter meetings and governance for initiated alumni brothers
  • Community service and civic engagement across Denton-Lewisville
  • Guide Right mentorship for young men through Kappa League
  • Scholarship support and educational programming

Guide Right Program

Kappa League: mentorship for young men

Guide Right is Kappa Alpha Psi's national mentorship initiative. Locally, our chapter runs it through Kappa League — structured programming that pairs young men with brothers of the chapter for leadership development, academic support, and exposure to opportunity beyond their everyday circle.

Leadership

Workshops and real responsibility that build young men who can lead in any room.

Academics

Support toward graduation and beyond, with brothers who've walked the path.

Brotherhood

Consistent, trusted adult mentorship rooted in the fraternity's founding principles.
