A Thriving Community

Growing Together in North Texas

The Denton–Lewisville area sits within one of the fastest-growing regions in the United States. Located in Denton County and connected to the greater Dallas–Fort Worth Metroplex, the area combines nationally recognized universities, thriving businesses, expanding residential communities, and abundant recreational opportunities.

As families, professionals, entrepreneurs, veterans, educators, and community leaders continue relocating to North Texas, the Denton-Lewisville (TX) Alumni Chapter serves as a fraternal home dedicated to achievement in every field of human endeavor while strengthening the communities we serve.
