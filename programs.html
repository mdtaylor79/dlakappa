Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to develop men, serve communities, and uplift youth across North Texas — guided by Leadership, Mentorship, Engagement, and Legacy.

Promoting literacy and a lifelong love of reading among youth through community partnerships and reading resources.

Academic support and enrichment helping students in Denton and Lewisville achieve their full scholastic potential.

A distinguished mentoring initiative offering young men opportunities for growth in leadership, academics, and community engagement.

Senior Kappa Affairs

Programs and outreach events supporting our senior brothers and the elderly members of our community.

Scholarship and philanthropic initiatives supporting educational achievement and community development.

Summer enrichment programs providing young men with mentorship, life skills, and character-building experiences.
