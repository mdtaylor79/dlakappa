The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter stands on a legacy of civic engagement, community service, and meaningful partnership across North Texas.

Our Commitment

Service is at the Heart of Who We Are

The Denton-Lewisville (TX) Alumni Chapter rests its shoulders on a deep and unwavering commitment to community. We place high emphasis on civic engagement — not as an obligation, but as a calling rooted in the very spirit of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc.

From neighborhood service projects and youth mentorship to civic partnerships and community-wide initiatives, our brothers show up where it matters most. We believe that true achievement extends beyond individual success — it is measured by the uplift we provide to those around us.

We actively seek and welcome partnerships with community leaders, local businesses, nonprofit organizations, and civic groups who share our values. Whether you want to serve alongside us or invite us to serve with you, there is a place at the table.

Ways to Serve With Us

Community Volunteer Days

Join brothers for scheduled days of service — neighborhood cleanups, food drives, school support events, and more across Denton and Lewisville.

Youth Mentorship

Volunteer as a mentor through our Guide Right and Kappa League programs, helping young men develop leadership skills, academic discipline, and character.

Civic Engagement

Participate in voter registration drives, civic forums, city council engagement, and community health initiatives that strengthen the fabric of North Texas.

Business & Corporate Partnerships

Partner with the chapter as a business or organization — sponsor events, co-host service projects, or connect your team with chapter volunteer opportunities.

Senior Community Support

Through our Senior Kappa Affairs program, volunteers assist elderly community members with resources, transportation coordination, and outreach events.

Literacy & Education

Support Room to Read and Achievement Academy efforts — assist with reading programs, tutoring events, and school supply drives in underserved communities.

Community Leaders & Business Partners Welcome

We actively encourage partnerships with community leaders, organizations, and businesses who share our passion for civic engagement and service. Whether you want to volunteer alongside our brothers or invite us to serve within your initiative, we are ready to build something meaningful together.

Strong communities are built through collaboration. The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter is committed to showing up as a reliable, dedicated partner in every endeavor we undertake.

Local Businesses

Sponsor a community event, co-host a service day, or connect your team with volunteer opportunities that give back to North Texas.

Community Organizations

Partner on outreach programs, youth initiatives, health fairs, and civic projects that strengthen our shared communities.

Civic & Government Leaders

Engage the chapter in civic forums, town halls, and community-wide initiatives that drive meaningful change across Denton and Lewisville.

Schools & Educational Institutions

Collaborate on mentorship, literacy, and after-school programs that prepare the next generation of leaders.
