Fraternity History

Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. was founded on January 5, 1911 at Indiana University — a legacy of achievement spanning more than a century.

A Century of Achievement

Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. was founded with one central purpose: Achievement. Since its founding in 1911, the Fraternity has never lost sight of its founding objective — "Achievement in Every Field of Human Endeavor."

From its roots at Indiana University, Kappa Alpha Psi has grown to encompass more than 700 chapters across the United States and internationally, uniting brothers in a shared commitment to scholarship, service, and the uplift of humanity.
