Chapter History

From its chartering in 1997 to the present, the Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter has built a lasting legacy across Denton and Lewisville, Texas.

Our Story

The Denton-Lewisville (TX) Alumni Chapter was chartered in 1997 with a mission to serve Denton and Lewisville through scholarship, mentorship, civic engagement, and brotherhood. In the decades since, the chapter has grown into one of North Texas's most respected community organizations.

Through programs like Guide Right, Kappa League, and the Achievement Academy, the chapter has touched thousands of lives — mentoring young men, supporting families, and partnering with local organizations to create lasting change.
