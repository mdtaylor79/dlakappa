The visionary brothers who established the Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter and set the standard for excellence across North Texas.

The Ten Founding Members

On January 5, 1911, ten young men at Indiana University in Bloomington, Indiana came together to found Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. — a brotherhood built on the principle of Achievement in Every Field of Human Endeavor. Their courage, vision, and sacrifice created a legacy that endures to this day.
