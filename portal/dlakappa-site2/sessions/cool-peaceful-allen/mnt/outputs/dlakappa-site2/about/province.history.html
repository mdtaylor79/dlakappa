Province History

The Southwestern Province encompasses Kappa Alpha Psi chapters across Texas and the surrounding region, united in service and achievement.

The Southwestern Province

The Southwestern Province of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. represents one of the Fraternity's most active and impactful provinces. Spanning Texas and neighboring states, the province includes dozens of graduate and undergraduate chapters committed to the Fraternity's mission.

The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter proudly serves as part of this distinguished province, contributing to provincial programs and initiatives while maintaining deep roots in our local North Texas communities.
