About — Leadership

Chapter Leadership

Guided by dedicated brothers committed to service and achievement, our leadership drives the chapter's mission forward.

Elected Officers

Polemarch

Duncan E. Brown — Chief executive officer of the chapter, responsible for overall leadership and strategic direction.

Vice Polemarch

Assists the Polemarch and assumes duties in his absence. Oversees chapter programs and initiatives.

Keeper of Records

Maintains all chapter records, correspondence, and official documentation.

Keeper of Exchequer

Manages chapter finances, budgets, and financial reporting.

Committee Chairs

Programs Committee

Coordinates all chapter community programs and service initiatives.

Membership Committee

Oversees membership intake, reclamation, and brother engagement.

Guide Right Committee

Leads youth mentorship programs and Kappa League activities.

Scholarship Committee

Administers scholarships and academic support initiatives.

Board Members

The chapter board provides strategic direction, governance, and accountability — ensuring the chapter fulfills its mission with excellence. Contact us at info@dlakappa.org to connect with chapter leadership.
