From the Polemarch

A word of welcome

Welcome to the official website of the Denton-Lewisville (TX) Alumni Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc.

It is our honor and privilege to welcome you to our digital home as we continue our commitment to service, achievement, and brotherhood throughout Denton and Lewisville, Texas. Our chapter proudly stands on the core tenets of Leadership, Mentorship, Engagement, and Legacy, which guide our mission and strengthen our impact within the communities we serve.

As members of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc., we remain dedicated to the fraternity’s ultimate objective of Achievement in Every Field of Human Endeavor. Through scholarship initiatives, youth mentorship, civic involvement, community partnerships, and professional development, we strive to uplift individuals while building stronger and more connected communities across North Texas.

We extend our sincere appreciation to our local leaders, community organizations, educational institutions, businesses, and supporters for their continued collaboration and partnership throughout Denton and Lewisville. Together, we are creating opportunities, inspiring future generations, and making a lasting difference through unity and service.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our programs, upcoming events, community outreach efforts, and the impactful work being accomplished by the Denton-Lewisville (TX) Alumni Chapter. Be sure to stay connected by following us across all social media platforms as we continue to expand our vision and demonstrate that all roads lead to the Denton-Lewisville (TX) Alumni Chapter.

Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to engaging with you as we continue serving our communities with purpose, excellence, and commitment.

Yours in the Bond,

Duncan E. Brown Polemarch · Denton-Lewisville (TX) Alumni Chapter
What guides us

Our four tenets

Every program and partnership we pursue answers to the principles that have carried this chapter since its founding.

L

Leadership

Developing men prepared to lead in their professions, families, and communities with conviction.

M

Mentorship

Guiding young men through Kappa League and Guide Right toward purpose and achievement.

E

Engagement

Showing up for North Texas with service projects, scholarships, and civic partnership.

L

Legacy

Honoring those who built this chapter and leaving the next generation better than we found them.
