About Us

The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter

Chartered in 1997, the Denton-Lewisville (TX) Alumni Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. stands as a pillar of service, scholarship, and achievement across North Texas.

Our Mission
Achievement in every field of human endeavor.

The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter was founded on the belief that brotherhood carries a responsibility — to serve, to lead, and to lift others. We show up for our communities in North Texas not because we have to, but because it is who we are.

What guides us

Our four tenets

Every program and partnership we pursue answers to the principles that have carried this chapter since its founding.

L

Leadership

Developing men prepared to lead in their professions, families, and communities with conviction.

M

Guiding young men through Kappa League and Guide Right toward purpose and achievement.
