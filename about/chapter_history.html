About — Chapter History

Chapter History

From its chartering in 1997 to the present, the Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter has built a lasting legacy across Denton and Lewisville, Texas.

Our Story

A Legacy of Achievement in North Texas

The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. was founded to extend the fraternity's legacy of achievement, brotherhood, and service into the North Texas region. Chartered in 1997, the chapter has grown into a cornerstone of community engagement, mentorship, and leadership development. Guided by the fraternity's ideals, the chapter has weathered challenges, celebrated milestones, and continually evolved to meet the needs of its members and the communities it serves.

Founding & Early Years

Chartered March 1, 1997

The chapter's journey began with the vision of a dedicated group of 16 alumni who sought to create a space for fellowship and service within Denton County. Officially chartered on March 1, 1997, the chapter was a beacon of fraternity ideals, fostering camaraderie and a commitment to achievement. Over the years, the chapter expanded its reach, embracing the Lewisville community to reflect its broader scope of impact.

1997

A Period of Reflection

2004 — Temporary Inactivity

In 2004, the chapter faced challenges that led to a temporary period of inactivity. However, the spirit of Kappa Alpha Psi remained alive in the hearts of its members. Through determination and collaboration, efforts to revive the chapter culminated in a triumphant reactivation in 2018, marking a new chapter in its storied history.

2004

Chapter enters a period of inactivity. The fraternal bond among members remains strong.

2018

Chapter reactivated — brothers reunite and welcome new members to carry the mission forward.

Reactivation & Resurgence

Renewed Purpose, Greater Impact

Since its reactivation, the chapter has focused on reclaiming members, welcoming new brothers, and revitalizing its community presence. Signature programs such as Guide Right and Kappa League have become hallmarks of the chapter's commitment to mentoring young men and fostering leadership. Scholarship initiatives, community service projects, and regional partnerships have further solidified the chapter's reputation as a leader in service and excellence.

Mission & Vision

Purpose-Driven Brotherhood

We are committed to achieving excellence in every field of human endeavor. Our mission is to cultivate leadership, foster brotherhood, and provide impactful service to our community. With a focus on mentorship, education, and civic engagement, the Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter seeks to inspire individuals and uplift communities, ensuring a legacy of positive impact for generations to come.

Looking Ahead

Building on Our Foundation

As we build on our foundation, the chapter remains dedicated to expanding its reach, strengthening its programs, and deepening its commitment to service. The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter continues to honor its past while embracing the opportunities of the future, striving to make a lasting difference in the lives of those we touch.
