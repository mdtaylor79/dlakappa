Membership

Join the Brotherhood

Membership in Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. is a lifelong commitment to achievement, service, and brotherhood. The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter welcomes men of character, integrity, and distinction.

Membership Opportunities

We offer two pathways for men who wish to join the Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter brotherhood.

New Interest

Are you a college graduate interested in becoming a member of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc.? We welcome men who share our commitment to scholarship, service, and achievement. Connect with us to learn about the membership process.

Submit Interest Form

Reclamation

Brothers who have become financially or otherwise inactive are encouraged to reclaim their membership and reconnect with the brotherhood. The Denton-Lewisville Alumni Chapter welcomes you back.

Submit Reclamation Form

Membership Requirements

Candidates must meet standards established by Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. and the Southwestern Province — including a college degree, demonstrated commitment to community service, and strong moral character.

To express interest, contact us at mta@dlakappa.org.
